Types of Leaders
- **Transformational Leaders** - aim to build up team morale and inspire change; use this type of leader when you want to foster innovation and motivation; *Steve Jobs, Gandhi*
- **Transactional Leaders** - focus on clear exchanges: do this and we will get this; effective for incentivizing goals but can be risky as rewards are costly; use this when clear structure and rewards are needed; *Jeff Bezos*
- **Servant Leaders** - prioritize the needs of the team, providing support and motivation; effective in nurturing team growth and collaboration; *Dalai Lama, Abraham Lincoln*
- **Autocratic Leaders** - make decisions independently without consulting the team; useful in crisis situations where quick decisions are necessary; *Donald Trump, Gordon Ramsay, Kim Jong Un*
- **Democratic Leaders** - actively seek input and feedback from team members before making decisions; ideal when team members are highly skilled and engaged; *Barack Obama, Indra Nooyi*

Responsibilities 
- Providing clear directions to guide the team
- Understanding the strengths and weaknesses of team members
- Organizing tasks and setting achievable goals
- Upholding the vision and mission of the group
- Solving problems and resolving conflicts effectively
